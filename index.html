    • What Makes Seiko so Special?

    (Seiko AGS Scuba 200m Kinetic Diver Titanium)

    Seiko is special because it has shown that watches don't have to be expensive enough to be iconic and unique. The Seiko SKX007, for example, is one of the best selling watches of all time and many people, including those who own expensive watches, consider it as a must-have watch due to its unique design.
